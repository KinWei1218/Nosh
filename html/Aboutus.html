About Nosh

Who We Are

Welcome to Nosh, where culinary exploration meets global adventure! We are a passionate team of food enthusiasts dedicated to bringing you the rich and diverse world of Malaysian and international cuisines. Our mission is to celebrate the vibrant flavors, unique ingredients, and traditional techniques that make food a universal language of culture and connection.


At Nosh, we believe that food is more than just sustenance—it's an experience that tells a story. Our website is a curated journey through Malaysia's culinary heritage, showcasing everything from street food delights to exquisite traditional dishes. We dive deep into the local food scenes, uncovering hidden gems and sharing authentic recipes that capture the essence of Malaysian cuisine.


But our journey doesn't stop there. We extend our culinary adventures beyond Malaysia's borders to explore global cuisines. Whether it's a beloved classic from Italy, an exotic dish from Thailand, or a comforting recipe from the Americas, we aim to provide a comprehensive guide to the world's most delightful dishes. Our goal is to inspire you to try new recipes, explore different food cultures, and embrace the joy of cooking.



Our History

Nosh was founded with a simple yet ambitious goal—to create a platform where food lovers can discover and enjoy the diverse culinary traditions of Malaysia and beyond. Our journey began in 2016, when a group of friends with a shared love for cooking and cultural exploration decided to combine their passions. What started as a small blog has grown into a vibrant community of food enthusiasts and experts. Through dedication and a love for food, we've expanded our reach, constantly adding new recipes, articles, and insights to help you savor every bite.



Commenting Policy

At Nosh, we value the exchange of ideas and experiences in our comments section. To ensure a positive and respectful environment, we ask that all comments adhere to the following guidelines:

  1. Be Respectful: Treat fellow commenters with kindness and respect.
  2. Stay On-Topic: Keep comments relevant to the content and discussion.
  3. No Spam: Avoid posting promotional or unrelated content.
  4. Use Constructive Feedback: Share your thoughts and opinions in a constructive manner.

Comments that violate these guidelines may be removed to maintain the integrity of our community. Thank you for contributing to a positive and engaging space!
