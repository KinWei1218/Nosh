Wajik

Wajik is a traditional Malay dessert that is sweet, sticky, and utterly satisfying. Made from glutinous rice, the dessert is cooked with palm sugar and coconut milk until it becomes soft and sticky, with a rich caramel-like flavor. The mixture is then pressed into a pan, cooled, and cut into diamond-shaped pieces.

Cook time

1 hour

Pax

4 pax

Tags: desserts

Instructions

Step 1

Steam the soaked glutinous rice until fully cooked.

Step 2

In a separate pan, cook coconut milk, palm sugar, and pandan leaf until thickened.

Step 3

Mix the cooked rice with the coconut milk mixture until well combined.

Step 4

Press the mixture into a pan and let it cool before cutting into squares.

Ingredients

2 cups glutinous rice, soaked 1 cup coconut milk 1 cup palm sugar, grated 1 pandan leaf
