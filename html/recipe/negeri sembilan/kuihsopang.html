Kuih Sopang

Kuih Sopang is a traditional Malay snack that is both sweet and chewy. Made from glutinous rice flour, the dough is filled with a mixture of grated coconut and palm sugar, creating a sweet, coconut-flavored filling. The filled dough is wrapped in banana leaves and steamed, infusing the kuih with a subtle, earthy aroma.

Cook time

50 minutes

Pax

4 pax

Tags: desserts

Instructions

Step 1

Cook grated coconut with palm sugar and water until the mixture thickens.

Step 2

Mix glutinous rice flour with water to form a dough.

Step 3

Fill the dough with the coconut mixture and wrap in banana leaves.

Step 4

Steam for 20 minutes until cooked.

Ingredients

2 cups glutinous rice flour 1 cup grated coconut 1/2 cup palm sugar 1/4 cup water Banana leaves for wrapping
