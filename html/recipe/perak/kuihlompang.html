Kuih Lompang

Kueh Lompang is a traditional Malay cake. It is usually the size of a small cup with a little dent or a small well in the middle. It is a sweet cake topped with steamed grated coconut that had been seasoned with little salt. This is to balance the sweetness of the cake and give it a creamy taste. It is a colorful cake.

Cook time

20 minutes

Pax

1 pax

Tags: desserts

Instructions

Step 1

In a mixing bowl, combine the rice flour, sugar, alkaline water, and pandan juice. Mix until smooth and free of lumps.

Step 2

Heat water in a steamer. Grease small molds or cups with a bit of oil.

Step 3

Pour the batter into the molds, filling them about 3/4 full. Steam for about 15 minutes, or until the kuih is firm.

Step 4

Let the kuih cool slightly before removing them from the molds.

Step 5

Top the kuih with grated coconut before serving.

Step 6

Serve as a sweet treat with tea or coffee.

Ingredients

250g rice flour, 150g sugar, 1/2 teaspoon alkaline water, 500ml water, 5 pandan leaves blended with 200ml water and strained, fresh grated coconut mixed with a pinch of salt
