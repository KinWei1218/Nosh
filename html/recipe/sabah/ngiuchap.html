Ngiu Chap

Ngiu Chap is a beloved noodle dish from Sabah, Malaysia, offering a rich and hearty eating experience. The dish features various cuts of beef, including brisket, tripe, and beef balls, all simmered in a deeply flavorful broth. This soup is often served with rice noodles, making it a comforting and filling meal.

Cook time

1.5 hours

Pax

4 pax

Tags: noodles meat

Instructions

Step 1

Simmer beef cuts in beef broth with soy sauce, garlic, and ginger until tender.

Step 2

Cook rice noodles according to package instructions.

Step 3

Serve the beef and broth over noodles, adding beef balls and green onions.

Ingredients

500g beef (various cuts like brisket, tripe) 1 liter beef broth 200g rice noodles 2 tbsp soy sauce 1 tbsp garlic, minced 1 tsp ginger, minced Beef balls Green onions, chopped
