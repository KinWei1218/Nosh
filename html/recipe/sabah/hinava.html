Hinava

Another traditional local dish that you should try when youâ€™re in Sabah is the Hinava,which originated from the Kadazan Dusun tribes. This dish consists of raw fish marinated with lime juice, which is mixed together with a variety of seasoning such as salt, red chilli, slice shallot and ginger. Think of it like eating sushi!

Cook time

50 minutes

Pax

2 pax

Tags: spicy salad

Instructions

Step 1

Slice the fish fillets thinly and place them in a mixing bowl.

Step 2

Squeeze the lime juice over the fish, ensuring all the pieces are covered. Let it marinate for at least 15 minutes.

Step 3

Add the finely chopped chilies, grated ginger, and sliced shallots to the fish.

Step 4

Add the julienned cucumber and mix well.

Step 5

Add salt to taste, mix well, and serve the Hinava chilled.

Ingredients

500g fresh mackerel fillets (or any white fish), 6-8 calamansi limes (or regular limes), 2 red chilies (finely chopped), 1 thumb-sized ginger (finely grated), 2 shallots (thinly sliced), 1 cucumber (julienned), salt to taste
