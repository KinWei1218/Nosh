Peranakan Laksa

Peranakan Laksa, also known as Nyonya Laksa, is a rich and aromatic noodle soup that is a staple in Peranakan cuisine. This dish blends Chinese and Malay culinary traditions, characterized by its creamy, coconut-based broth and vibrant, spicy flavors.

Cook time

35 minutes

Pax

1 pax

Tags: spicy noodles

Instructions

Step 1

In a pot, combine laksa paste, chicken broth, and coconut milk. Bring to a simmer.

Step 2

Add shredded chicken, fish sauce, and sugar. Cook for about 10 minutes.

Step 3

Cook rice noodles according to package instructions and drain.

Step 4

Place noodles in bowls, ladle the laksa soup over them, and garnish with bean sprouts and fresh herbs.

Ingredients

200g rice noodles, 300g chicken (shredded), 200ml coconut milk, 2 tablespoons laksa paste, 1 cup chicken broth, 2 tablespoons fish sauce, 1 tablespoon sugar, bean sprouts and fresh herbs for garnish
