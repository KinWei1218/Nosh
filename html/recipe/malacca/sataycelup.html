Satay Celup

Satay Celup is a unique dish from Melaka, Malaysia, where various skewered meats, seafood, and vegetables are cooked fondue-style in a communal pot of thick, spicy satay sauce. The sauce is made from ground peanuts, chili, and various spices, creating a rich and flavorful dip that perfectly complements the grilled skewers.

Cook time

40 minutes

Pax

3 pax

Tags: seafood meat

Instructions

Step 1

Prepare the satay sauce by boiling peanuts, chili paste, tamarind juice, sugar, and salt in water until thickened.

Step 2

Heat the sauce in a pot until boiling.

Step 3

Dip skewered ingredients into the boiling sauce and cook until done.

Ingredients

Various skewered meats and vegetables (e.g., chicken, tofu, mushrooms) 1 cup peanuts, roasted and ground 2 tbsp chili paste 1 tbsp tamarind juice 2 tbsp sugar Salt to taste
