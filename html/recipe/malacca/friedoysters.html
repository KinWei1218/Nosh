Fried Oyster

Fried Oysters, or "Oyster Omelette," is a popular street food in many parts of Asia. The dish features fresh oysters mixed with a light, starchy batter, then fried until golden and crispy. Itâ€™s often seasoned with garlic, chili, and soy sauce, giving it a savory and slightly spicy flavor. The oysters remain tender and juicy inside the crispy batter, making this dish a delightful contrast of textures.

Cook time

20 minutes

Pax

2 pax

Tags: seafood

Instructions

Step 1

Mix flour, cornstarch, egg, salt, and pepper to form a batter.

Step 2

Dip the oysters into the batter and fry in hot oil until golden brown.

Step 3

Serve with a dipping sauce.

Ingredients

200g fresh oysters 1/2 cup flour 1/4 cup cornstarch 1 egg Salt and pepper to taste Oil for frying
