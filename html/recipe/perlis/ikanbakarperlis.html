Ikan Bakar Perlis

Ikan Bakar Perlis is a beloved grilled fish dish from the state of Perlis, Malaysia. This dish is known for its aromatic and flavorful marinade, which typically includes ingredients such as tamarind, turmeric, lemongrass, and etc. The fish is usually wrapped in banana leaves before grilling, which not only infuses the fish with a subtle, earthy aroma but also keeps it moist and tender during the grilling process.

Cook time

1 hour

Pax

4 pax

Tags: spicy seafood

Instructions

Step 1

Mix the tamarind paste, turmeric powder, salt, and oil in a bowl

Step 2

Clean the fish and make a few slits on both sides.

Step 3

Rub the marinade all over the fish, including inside the slits. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 4

Preheat the grill or barbecue.

Step 5

Wrap the marinated fish in banana leaves for extra flavor and grill until the fish is cooked through, about 15 minutes on each side.

Step 6

Serve the grilled fish with rice and sambal on the side.

Ingredients

1 whole fish (e.g., stingray, mackerel), 2 tablespoons tamarind paste, 2 tablespoons turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon salt, 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, banana leaves (optional, for wrapping)
