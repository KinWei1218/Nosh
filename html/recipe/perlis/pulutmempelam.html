Pulut Mempelam

Pulut Mempelam is a classic Malaysian dessert that combines the rich, sticky texture of glutinous rice (pulut) with the sweet and tangy flavor of ripe mangoes (mempelam). Itâ€™s a dish that beautifully highlights the natural sweetness of the mangoes, paired with the creamy richness of coconut milk-infused sticky rice.

Cook time

45 minutes

Pax

2 pax

Tags: desserts

Instructions

Step 1

Steam the soaked glutinous rice until fully cooked.

Step 2

In a saucepan, combine coconut milk, sugar, and salt. Heat gently, stirring until the sugar dissolves. Do not let it boil.

Step 3

Pour the coconut milk mixture over the steamed glutinous rice, stirring to combine.

Step 4

Serve the rice with sliced mangoes on top.

Ingredients

2 cups glutinous rice (soaked overnight), 1 cup thick coconut milk, 2 tablespoons sugar, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 2 ripe mangoes (sliced)
