Jagung Kelapa Parut

Jagung Kelapa Parut is a simple yet delicious Malaysian dessert that combines the natural sweetness of corn with the rich, nutty flavor of freshly grated coconut. The dish is made by mixing steamed sweet corn with grated coconut, sugar, and a pinch of salt, creating a light and refreshing snack.

Cook time

10 minutes

Pax

2 pax

Tags: desserts

Instructions

Step 1

Mix cooked sweet corn with grated coconut, sugar, and salt.

Step 2

Serve immediately as a dessert or snack.

Ingredients

2 cups sweet corn (cooked) 1 cup grated coconut 2 tbsp sugar A pinch of salt
