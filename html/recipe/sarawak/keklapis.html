Kek Lapis

Kek Lapis, or layer cake, is an intricate and visually stunning dessert that is especially popular during festive occasions like Hari Raya in Malaysia. The cake is made by meticulously layering colorful batters, one on top of the other, and baking each layer individually.

Cook time

2 hours

Pax

2 pax

Tags: desserts

Instructions

Step 1

Cream butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.

Step 2

Add eggs one at a time, followed by flour and vanilla extract.

Step 3

Divide the batter into different bowls and add different food colors.

Step 4

Layer the batter in a baking pan, baking each layer separately at 180Â°C until set.

Ingredients

250g butter 250g sugar 10 eggs 200g flour 1 tsp vanilla extract Food coloring (various colors)
