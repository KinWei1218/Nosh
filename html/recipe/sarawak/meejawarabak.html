Mee Jawa Rabak

Mee Jawa Rabak is a hearty and flavorful noodle dish with roots in Javanese cuisine. It features yellow egg noodles served in a thick, savory gravy made from a blend of spices, tomatoes, and sometimes peanut sauce. The dish is typically topped with slices of tender beef or chicken, boiled eggs, and garnished with fried shallots and green onions.

Cook time

45 minutes

Pax

1 pax

Tags: noodles

Instructions

Step 1

Cook the noodles according to package instructions.

Step 2

Stir-fry the meat with curry powder, onion, and garlic until cooked.

Step 3

Add tomato sauce and some water to create a thick sauce.

Step 4

Serve the noodles with the sauce, boiled eggs, and garnishes.

Ingredients

200g yellow noodles 200g beef or chicken 2 tbsp curry powder 1 onion, chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 2 tbsp tomato sauce 2 boiled eggs Fried shallots and green onions for garnish
