Sarawak Kueh Chap

It is actually a mix of Flat Rice Noodles (known as Kueh), in a pork stew consisting of various pork innards and different parts of pork meats - where the ‘Chap’ literally translate to Mix."

Cook time

5 hours

Pax

3 pax

Tags: soup meat

Instructions

Step 1

Mix rice flour with water to form a smooth batter. Pour into a steamer and steam for about 15 minutes until set. Cut into pieces.

Step 2

In a pot, heat oil and sauté garlic until fragrant.

Step 3

Add pork belly slices and cook until browned.

Step 4

Pour in the broth, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and five-spice powder. Simmer for 15 minutes.

Step 5

Add tofu slices and cook for an additional 5 minutes.

Step 6

Serve the pork and tofu mixture over the steamed rice flour pieces. Garnish with fresh coriander.

Ingredients

200g rice flour, 1 litre chicken or pork broth, 200g pork belly (sliced), 100g tofu (sliced), 2 cloves garlic (chopped), 1 tablespoon soy sauce, 1 tablespoon oyster sauce, 1 teaspoon five-spice powder, fresh coriander for garnish
