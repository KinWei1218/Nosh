Sarawak Laksa

Sarawak Laksa is a noodle soup that is made up of several components, each with its unique flavour and texture. The soup is typically made using a combination of chicken and prawn stock, which gives it a rich and savoury flavour.

Cook time

20 minutes

Pax

1 pax

Tags: spicy noodles

Instructions

Step 1

Cook the rice vermicelli according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

Step 2

In a pot, heat a little oil and saut√© the Sarawak Laksa paste until fragrant.

Step 3

Add the chicken broth and bring to a boil.

Step 4

Stir in the coconut milk, fish sauce, and soy sauce. Simmer for 5 minutes.

Step 5

Add the cooked chicken and prawns to the soup. Cook until the prawns are pink.

Step 6

Season with salt and sugar to taste.

Step 7

Garnish with bean sprouts, boiled egg halves, fresh coriander, and lime wedges.

Ingredients

200g rice vermicelli, 200g cooked chicken (shredded), 200g prawns (peeled and deveined), 1 cup bean sprouts, 4 boiled eggs (halved), 2 cups chicken broth, 3 tablespoons Sarawak Laksa paste, 1 cup coconut milk, 2 tablespoons fish sauce, 1 tablespoon soy sauce, fresh coriander and lime wedges for garnish
