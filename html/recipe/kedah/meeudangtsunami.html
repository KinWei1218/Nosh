Mee Udang Tsunami

Mee Udang Tsunami is a bold and flavorful noodle dish, known for its generous serving of prawns and rich, spicy broth. The dish features yellow noodles served in a thick, prawn-based broth that is infused with chili paste, garlic, and various spices. The prawns are cooked until tender and juicy, making them the star of the dish.

Cook time

45 minutes

Pax

2 pax

Tags: seafood noodles

Instructions

Step 1

Cook the noodles according to package instructions.

Step 2

Stir-fry the prawns with chili paste, onion, and garlic.

Step 3

Add prawn stock and simmer to create a thick broth.

Step 4

Serve the broth over noodles, garnished with bean sprouts and boiled egg.

Ingredients

200g yellow noodles 300g prawns, cleaned 2 tbsp chili paste 1 onion, chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 cup prawn stock Bean sprouts and boiled egg for garnish
