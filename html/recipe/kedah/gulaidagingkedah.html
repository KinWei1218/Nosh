Gulai Daging Kedah

Gulai Daging Kedah is a traditional Malay dish from the Kedah state of Malaysia, known for its rich and aromatic flavors. This dish is a type of curry made with tender beef, simmered slowly in a fragrant blend of spices, coconut milk, and tamarind, creating a luscious and savory sauce. It is often enjoyed during special occasions and festive gatherings, reflecting the rich culinary heritage of Kedah.

Cook time

2 hours

Pax

3 pax

Tags: spicy meat

Instructions

Step 1

Heat the cooking oil in a pot and saut√© the minced garlic, shallots, lemongrass, cinnamon stick, cloves, cardamom pods, and star anise until fragrant.

Step 2

Add the curry powder and turmeric powder, stir well, and cook for about 2 minutes.

Step 3

Add the beef cubes and stir until the beef is well coated with the spice mixture.

Step 4

Pour in enough water to cover the beef, add the tamarind slice, and let it simmer until the beef is tender.

Step 5

Add the coconut milk and continue to simmer for another 15-20 minutes until the gravy thickens.

Step 6

Season with salt and sugar to taste.

Step 7

Serve hot with steamed rice.

Ingredients

500g beef (cut into cubes), 200ml coconut milk, 2 tablespoons cooking oil, 3 tablespoons curry powder (meat), 1 tablespoon turmeric powder, 4 cloves garlic (chopped), 3 shallots, minced, 2 stalks lemongrass (bruised), 1 piece of tamarind slice, 1 piece cinnamon stick, 3 cloves, 2 cardamom pods, 1 star anise, salt and sugar to taste, water
