Lontong Kering

A traditional dish that combines tender rice cakes (lontong) with sambal goreng, bergedil (potato patties), serunding (spiced grated coconut), and your choice of sambal sotong, beef rendang, crispy fried chicken, or paru goreng (beef lung). Each mouthful promises a burst of authentic flavours that will leave you craving for more.

Cook time

20 minutes

Pax

5 pax

Tags: rice

Instructions

Step 1

Cut the lontong rice cakes into bite-sized pieces and set aside.

Step 2

Heat oil in a pot and saut√© the garlic, shallots, and chili paste until fragrant.

Step 3

Add the beef slices and cook until browned.

Step 4

Stir in the turmeric powder, curry powder, and dried shrimp. Cook until the oil separates.

Step 5

Pour in the coconut milk and tamarind juice. Let it simmer until the beef is tender.

Step 6

6. Season with salt and sugar to taste.

Step 7

Serve the lontong with the beef curry and various side dishes.

Ingredients

1 pack of lontong rice cakes (store-bought or homemade), 200g of sliced beef, 2 cups coconut milk, 2 tablespoons chili paste, 1 tablespoon turmeric powder, 1 tablespoon curry powder, 1 cup of dried shrimp (soaked and blended), 4 cloves garlic (chopped), 2 shallots (chopped), 2 tablespoons tamarind juice, salt and sugar to taste, garnishes (serunding, sambal goreng, boiled eggs, and fried tempeh)
