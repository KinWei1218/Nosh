Nasi Kukus

Nasi Kukus Ayam Rempah is a Malaysian favorite, often found at roadside stalls and food markets. This dish features fluffy, steamed rice paired with crispy, spiced fried chicken that is marinated with a blend of aromatic spices like turmeric, cumin, and coriander. The chicken is deep-fried to perfection, resulting in a crispy exterior and juicy, tender meat inside.

Cook time

50 minutes

Pax

1 pax

Tags: rice spicy meat

Instructions

Step 1

Marinate the chicken with curry powder, turmeric, garlic, onion, and salt. Let it sit for 30 minutes.

Step 2

Steam the rice until fully cooked.

Step 3

Fry the marinated chicken until golden brown.

Step 4

Serve the chicken with steamed rice, curry sauce, and sambal.

Ingredients

2 cups rice 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces 3 tbsp curry powder 2 tbsp turmeric powder 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 onion, chopped Salt to taste Oil for frying Curry sauce and sambal for serving
