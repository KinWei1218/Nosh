Kerabu Kaki Ayam

Kerabu Kaki Ayam, or Chicken Feet Salad, is a distinctive and flavorful dish popular in Kelantan. This traditional Malaysian salad features chicken feet as the main ingredient, which are simmered until tender, then combined with a tangy, spicy dressing and fresh vegetables. Itâ€™s a refreshing and spicy salad that showcases the unique textures and flavors of Malaysian cuisine.

Cook time

1.25 hours

Pax

2 pax

Tags: Spicy Meat

Instructions

Step 1

Boil the chicken feet until tender, then peel off the skin and cut into bite-sized pieces.

Step 2

Mix lime juice, fish sauce, and sugar until well combined.

Step 3

Combine the chicken feet, red onion, cucumber, cilantro, and red chili in a bowl.

Step 4

Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to mix well.

Ingredients

500g of chicken feet, 1 red onion (thinly sliced), 1 cucumber (thinly sliced), 1 handful of fresh cilantro (chopped), 1 red chili (sliced), 2 tablespoons of lime juice, 1 tablespoon of fish sauce, 1 tablespoon of sugar
