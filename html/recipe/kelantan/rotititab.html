Roti Titab

Roti Titab is a popular breakfast dish from Kelantan, Malaysia, known for its simple yet delicious combination of ingredients. It consists of thick slices of toasted bread, generously spread with butter and kaya (a sweet coconut jam), and topped with perfectly cooked half-boiled eggs. The soft yolks mix with the sweet kaya and butter, creating a rich and flavorful experience.

Cook time

10 minutes

Pax

3 pax

Tags: breakfast

Instructions

Step 1

Toast the bread slices.

Step 2

Spread butter and kaya on each slice.

Step 3

Cook the eggs until half-boiled and place one on each toast.

Step 4

Serve immediately.

Ingredients

4 slices of bread 2 tbsp kaya 2 eggs Butter for spreading
