Ketupat Sotong

Ketupat Sotong is a traditional Malay dish that offers a delightful combination of textures and flavors. In this dish, squids are meticulously stuffed with glutinous rice, which has been soaked and flavored, before being simmered in a sweet and creamy coconut milk sauce. The squid becomes tender while the rice absorbs the rich, coconut flavors, creating a unique, satisfying dish.

Cook time

1 hour

Pax

3 pax

Tags: seafood rice

Instructions

Step 1

Stuff the soaked glutinous rice into the cleaned squid.

Step 2

Simmer the stuffed squid in coconut milk with pandan leaf, sugar, and salt until the rice is cooked and the squid is tender.

Step 3

Serve warm.

Ingredients

500g squid 1 cup glutinous rice, soaked 2 cups coconut milk 1 pandan leaf 1 tbsp sugar Salt to taste
