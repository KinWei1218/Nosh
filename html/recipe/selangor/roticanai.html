Roti Canai

Roti Canai is a popular Malaysian flatbread with Indian origins, renowned for its crispy and flaky texture. Itâ€™s a staple in Malaysian cuisine, often enjoyed as a breakfast dish or as a snack throughout the day. The bread is traditionally served with dhal (lentil curry), chicken curry, or other types of curry.

2 minutes

Pax

1 pax

Tags: Breakfast

Instructions

Step 1

Mix flour, salt, sugar, and yeast. Gradually add warm water, knead until smooth, and let rest in a covered bowl for 2 hours (or overnight).

Step 2

Divide dough into 8-10 portions, flatten each into discs, brush with ghee/oil, and stack. Let rest for 30 minutes.

Step 3

Heat a skillet. Stretch each dough portion thin, fold into a rectangle, and cook in the skillet over medium heat for 1-2 minutes per side until golden and crispy.

Step 4

Serve hot with curry or dhal.

Ingredients

3 cups all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 tablespoon sugar, 1 teaspoon instant yeast, 1 cup warm water (approx.), 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter) or vegetable oil, extra oil for cooking
