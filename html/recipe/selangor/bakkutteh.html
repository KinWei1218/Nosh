Bak Kut Teh

Bak Kut Teh is a traditional Chinese-Malaysian soup that translates to "meat bone tea." It is a comforting and aromatic dish made by simmering pork ribs in a complex broth infused with a mix of Chinese herbs and spices such as star anise, cinnamon, and garlic. The soup is rich in flavor, with a deep umami taste, and is typically served with rice, tofu, and a side of pickled vegetables.

Cook time

2 hours

Pax

4 pax

Tags: meat soup

Instructions

Step 1

Simmer the pork ribs in water with Bak Kut Teh spices and garlic for about 1.5 hours.

Step 2

Add soy sauce, dark soy sauce, and salt to taste.

Step 3

Serve hot with rice.

Ingredients

500g pork ribs 2 liters water 1 pack Bak Kut Teh spices 5 cloves garlic, smashed 2 tbsp soy sauce 1 tsp dark soy sauce Salt to taste
