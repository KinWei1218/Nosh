Satay

Satay is one of Southeast Asiaâ€™s most iconic dishes, featuring skewered and grilled meat, usually chicken, beef, or lamb, marinated in a flavorful mix of spices. The skewers are grilled over an open flame, giving the meat a smoky, charred exterior while keeping it juicy inside. Satay is traditionally served with a rich and creamy peanut sauce, along with sides like rice cakes (ketupat), cucumber slices, and onions.

Cook time

1 hours

Pax

4 pax

Tags: meat

Instructions

Step 1

Marinate the meat with turmeric, cumin, coriander, garlic, onion, and soy sauce for at least 2 hours.

Step 2

Thread the meat onto skewers and grill until cooked through.

Step 3

Serve with peanut sauce and rice cakes.

Ingredients

500g chicken or beef, cut into cubes 2 tbsp turmeric powder 1 tbsp cumin powder 1 tbsp coriander powder 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 onion, chopped 2 tbsp soy sauce Skewers
