Labu Masak Lemak

Labu Masak Lemak is a beloved dish from Pahang, Malaysia, characterized by its rich, creamy, and slightly spicy coconut gravy. The dish primarily features pumpkin (labu) cooked in a luscious coconut milk base, often enhanced with aromatic herbs and spices. It's a traditional Malay recipe that showcases the use of local ingredients to create a comforting and flavorful meal.

Cook time

40 minutes

Pax

2 pax

Tags: desserts

Instructions

Step 1

In a pot, combine coconut milk, lemongrass, and bird’s eye chilies. Bring to a simmer.

Step 2

Add the pumpkin cubes and cook until they are tender.

Step 3

Stir in the shredded turmeric leaf and continue cooking for a few more minutes.

Step 4

Season with salt and sugar to taste.

Step 5

Serve hot with rice.

Ingredients

500g pumpkin (peeled and cut into cubes), 2 cups coconut milk, 2 stalks lemongrass (smashed), 2 bird’s eye chilies (smashed), 1 turmeric leaf (shredded), salt and sugar to taste
