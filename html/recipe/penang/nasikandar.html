Nasi Kandar

Nasi Kandar is a popular dish from Penang, Malaysia, characterized by its diverse and flavorful spread. The dish consists of steamed rice, often served with an array of curries, vegetables, fried chicken, and other side dishes. The rice is typically plain, allowing it to soak up the rich and aromatic curry sauces.
