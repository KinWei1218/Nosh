Chendol

Chendol is a refreshing Malaysian dessert that is perfect for cooling down on a hot day. It consists of green jelly noodles made from rice flour, served in a bowl of cold coconut milk and sweetened with palm sugar syrup. The dessert often includes additional ingredients like red beans, sweet corn, and shaved ice. Instructions

Step 1

Boil the palm sugar with a little water to create a syrup.

Step 2

In a serving bowl, add the chendol, red beans, and crushed ice.

Step 3

Pour coconut milk and palm sugar syrup over the top and serve immediately.

Ingredients

1 cup chendol (green jelly noodles) 1 cup coconut milk 1/2 cup palm sugar 1/2 cup red beans (optional) Crushed ice
