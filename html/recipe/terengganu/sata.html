Sata

Sata is a traditional dish from Terengganu, Malaysia, made from fish paste mixed with grated coconut, chili, and spices, then wrapped in banana leaves and grilled. The grilling process imparts a smoky flavor to the fish, while the banana leaves keep it moist and tender. Sata is often served as a snack or appetizer and is cherished for its savory, spicy taste and aromatic presentation.

Cook time

40 minutes

Pax

2 pax

Tags: seafood

Instructions

Step 1

Blend fish, onion, garlic, chili paste, and turmeric powder into a smooth paste.

Step 2

Spoon the paste onto banana leaves, fold, and secure.

Step 3

Grill the wrapped fish paste until cooked through.

Step 4

Serve hot.

Ingredients

200g fish fillet 1 onion, chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 tbsp chili paste 1 tsp turmeric powder Banana leaves for wrapping
